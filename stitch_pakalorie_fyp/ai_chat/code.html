Is Nihari healthy?

YOU • 09:42 AM

Nihari Insights

Traditional Nihari is rich in protein and collagen, but it's typically high in saturated fats and sodium. Here's a typical breakdown per 250g serving:

Calories 540 kcal
Protein 38g
Fats 32g
Sodium 1,200mg
eco

LIGHTER ALTERNATIVE

Try Paya Soup or a slow-cooked Lean Beef Stew with minimal oil and whole-wheat tandoori roti for a similar depth of flavor with 40% less fat.
